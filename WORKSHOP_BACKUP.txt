----------------------------------------------------------------
  BACKGROUND: THE INCIDENT
----------------------------------------------------------------

New Year's Day, 1 January 2024. A mass police response descends
on Bayside Marketplace in Miami, Florida. Over 60 officers are
deployed. Roads are closed. Helicopters circle overhead.

Official explanation: a teenage brawl.

What went viral online: dozens of eyewitness videos appear to
show 8-10 foot tall "Shadow Beings" moving through the mall's
glass. The footage spreads globally within hours. Governments
and Miami police decline to comment on the creature claims.
The story disappears from mainstream media within days.

The online community never let it go.

----------------------------------------------------------------
  BACKGROUND: JOSHUA PETER — YOUR INTERVIEW SUBJECT
----------------------------------------------------------------

Name:         Joshua Peter, age 32
From:         Hialeah, Florida
Background:   Former blue-collar worker

Joshua was at Bayside Marketplace on the night of the incident.
He claims to have witnessed "Shadow Beings" physically phase
through the mall's glass in front of him.

In the weeks after 1 January 2024, his life unravelled. He lost
his job, became estranged from family, and began obsessively
documenting what he saw. By 2025 he had self-published a
survivalist account alleging a coordinated government cover-up
and what he calls a "containment failure."

Now in 2026, Joshua is a deeply cynical and paranoid source.
He claims to hold a file — DSC_0042_RAW_RECOVERED.mov — stored
on an air-gapped device, which he says is uncorrupted digital
evidence the public has never seen.

He is especially haunted by "Fitz" — a fellow witness from that
night who subsequently vanished. Joshua believes Fitz was
removed by federal tactical teams. Police have not investigated.

Today is the first time Joshua has agreed to speak to an
outside journalist.

Your job: find out if the evidence is real — or if Joshua
himself is the story.

----------------------------------------------------------------
  EXERCISE 02 — INTERVIEW: JOSHUA PETER
----------------------------------------------------------------

You are now going to conduct a live AI interview with Joshua.
Go to the link below and select Joshua Peter when prompted.

  Link: https://1410-2a00-23c6-9d4d-4201-6013-7878-6017-a6db.ngrok-free.app

Use the three goals below to guide your conversation. You do
not have to follow them in order — let Joshua lead, then
steer back when you need to.

  - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
  GOAL 01 — Secure Physical Evidence
  - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

  Establish custody of the footage before anything else.

  Suggested questions:
  1. "Where is DSC_0042_RAW_RECOVERED.mov physically stored
     right now? Who else has access?"
  2. "Walk me through the first 30 seconds of that footage."
  3. "Has anyone else seen this file since January 2024?
     Has it been copied?"
  4. "Why did you air-gap the device?"

  - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
  GOAL 02 — Verify Technical Data
  - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

  Push on the specifics — data doesn't lie, but sources do.

  Suggested questions:
  1. "You mention GPS logs. What device recorded them and
     what do the coordinates show?"
  2. "14Hz is below the threshold of human hearing. What
     equipment captured that audio?"
  3. "People near infrasound often report dread or unease.
     Did you experience that?"
  4. "Has anyone with technical expertise independently
     reviewed your data?"

  - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
  GOAL 03 — Trace the Disappeared: Fitz
  - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

  A missing witness changes everything.

  Suggested questions:
  1. "Full name, how you knew each other, last confirmed
     contact with Fitz."
  2. "Was Fitz with you at Bayside that night?"
  3. "What was Fitz's account of what they saw?
     Did it match yours?"
  4. "When exactly did Fitz go dark? Did they say
     anything before?"
  5. "Have police been notified? What was their response?"
